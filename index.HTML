About Karabo Hospital

Established in 1995, Karabo Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare delivery in Pretoria and surrounding areas. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in medical care.

We combine state-of-the-art technology with compassionate care to provide comprehensive medical services to our diverse community.

Our Story

Karabo Hospital was founded in 1995 with a vision to provide world-class healthcare services to the people of Pretoria and surrounding communities. Over the years, we have grown from a small clinic to a full-service hospital with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, high-quality healthcare services that meet the needs of our diverse community through clinical excellence, innovative technology, and a commitment to patient-centered care.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy, dignity, and respect.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of patient care.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and treatments to improve patient outcomes.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our interactions.
  • Community: We are committed to serving and improving the health of our community.

Comprehensive Medical Services

At Karabo Hospital, we offer a wide range of specialized medical services provided by experienced healthcare professionals.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care with state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment facilities for all cardiac conditions.

  • Cardiac Catheterization
  • Echocardiography
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation
  • Pacemaker Implantation

Maternity & Pediatrics

Specialized care for mothers and children, from prenatal services to pediatric healthcare.

  • Prenatal and Postnatal Care
  • Labor and Delivery
  • Neonatal Intensive Care
  • Pediatric Vaccinations

Orthopedics

Expert diagnosis and treatment for bone, joint, and muscle conditions with advanced surgical options.

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Arthroscopic Procedures
  • Fracture Care
  • Sports Medicine

Neurology

Comprehensive care for disorders of the nervous system with cutting-edge diagnostic technology.

  • EEG and EMG Testing
  • Stroke Treatment
  • Epilepsy Management
  • Neurological Rehabilitation

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care services from routine vision checks to complex surgical procedures.

  • Cataract Surgery
  • Glaucoma Treatment
  • Retinal Procedures
  • Laser Vision Correction

General Surgery

Expert surgical care for a wide range of conditions with minimally invasive techniques.

  • Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Gastrointestinal Procedures
  • Hernia Repair
  • Thyroid Surgery
